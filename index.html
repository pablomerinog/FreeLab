Frequently Asked Questions

What is cryopreservation?

Cryopreservation is the process of preserving cells, tissues, or organs at extremely low temperatures to maintain their viability for future use.

How long can biological materials be stored?

When stored properly at cryogenic temperatures, biological materials can be preserved indefinitely without degradation.

Is cryopreservation safe?

Yes, cryopreservation is a scientifically proven and safe method for preserving biological materials.

What types of materials can be cryopreserved?

We can preserve cells, tissues, organs, and genetic material such as DNA and RNA.

How do I get started with FreeLab?

Contact us through our website or visit our lab to discuss your preservation needs with our experts.
